MEANING AND BRIEF ABOUT PSYCHOLOGY

WHAT IS PSYCHOLOGY?

Psychology is a brief study about mind and behaviour. It explores the way people think, act depending upon the circumstance, perception and memory. Psychology isn't always about reading an individual's mind or just mental health but psychology has various branches with deal with a particular thing. Psychology is also useful to help an individual deal with few complex and negative obstacles in life. It connects with the real-world problems. Modern Psychology was discovered by Wilhelm Wundt in 1879 in a laboratory.
